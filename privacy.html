Privacy Policy – Aura Architect

Last updated: 2025-12-30

Aura Architect processes data to enable optional integration with third-party platforms (e.g., TikTok) via user authorization (OAuth), and to operate and troubleshoot the integration.

Data We May Process

  • Basic account identifiers returned by the connected platform after authorization
  • Authorization tokens (stored securely for the purpose of the integration)
  • Technical logs (timestamps, request status, error messages)

Purpose

  • Enable the requested connection and content transfer functionality
  • Troubleshoot and improve reliability
  • Prevent misuse and protect the service

Sharing

Data is shared only with the connected platform as part of the integration flow and with service providers strictly necessary to run the service (if any). We do not sell personal data.

Retention & Control

  • Tokens and logs are kept only as long as needed for the integration, or until you revoke access.
  • You can revoke authorization at any time through the connected platform settings.
  • You can request deletion of stored data by contacting us.
